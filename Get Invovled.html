How You Can Get Involved

As a guest

Our connection to Pillow Partners, who has fantastic guests have helped support our charity endlessly. There are so many ways guests of Pillow can help and support the charity and our causes. Guests can also get involved in our fantastic fundraisers, virtual events or even start a fundraiser for something they care about with our support.

  • 01 Donate at checkout when booking your property on Pillow Partners
  • 02 Leave non-perishable food, clothes, books or games when you checkout
  • 03 Donate at any point during your stay by scanning in-property QR codes
  • 04 Book one of Pillow Partners special Givecation packages
How You Can Get Involved

As a property owner

The kindness of property owners have been a true rock in being able to provide safe spaces for our charity. Safe havens and those that have used the properties provided think you are amazing for giving time away to families and individuals that need to have that much needed break away from it all. We are always building our network of Safe Havens across the UK and could not have provided these safety nets for people without your support.

  • 01 Pledge empty nights in your holiday home to gift
  • 02 Donate 1% of income monthly (Clients of Pillow Partners only)
  • 03 Lease your property to Pillow to allow us to add to our charity network
How You Can Get Involved

As Individuals

Individuals make the world go round, and your collective of individual actions truly make a huge difference to what we do. Wether it is donating, taking part, virtual events, Give-Cations, you are always there to make things happen and support those that need a little help.

  • 01 Join our monthly Virtual fundraising activities
  • 02 donate non-perishable food, clothes, books or games
  • 03 Join in on our fundraising activities
