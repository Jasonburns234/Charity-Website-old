About us

We care about causes

We want to help support other charities and causes that you care about as individuals in the community. We actively encourage inclusion and are here to help what matters to you.

Pillow Safe Havens started as a network of safe and relaxing escapes across the UK where guests can heal, rest, reset and make memories. We wanted to help create something amazing for those who need it the most. All our Safe Space Retreats are funded by Pillow Partners and their amazing property owners, generous individuals, and corporate partners

We continue to support out in the communities, raise funds for charities and complete fundraisers across the UK for great causes. Anyone can get involved by staring a fundraiser or donating to support our current and ongoing projects.

How we work

We exist because we are needed.

  • 01We raise money from different sources

    Raising charity money for Safe Havens involves seeking funding from various sources such as individuals, corporations, grants, events, and online campaigns.

  • 02We provide relief to areas all over the world

    We have no bias, we want to help support what you care about no matter what part of the world needs the support.

  • 03Our network stretches all across the UK

    We have a fantastic team of dedicated volunteers all over the UK who all make what we do a safe havens possible.
