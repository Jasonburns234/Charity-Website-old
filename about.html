About us

A Bit About Pillow Safe Havens

Pillow Safe Havens is the official charity of Pillow Partners

Our mission is to provide funded experiences every year in and around our properties across the UK for those in society that need it the most.

We focus on local charities and good causes within local communities. To these smaller, lesser-know charities, every single donation matters. The money stays locally and helps to reach people in those communities that need it the most.

Whatever you care about, we will have a local cause you can support.

Safe Havens

Discover our Amazing Safe Havens

With the Support of Pillow Partners we are able to gift time in our Safe Havens to provide a break for individuals and families to get away from the strains of life. We receive applications, nominations from friends and family, or referrals from our charity partners for those wishing to use the facilities.
