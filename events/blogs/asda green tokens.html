Safe Havens Win Asda Green Token Programme

Asda’s Green Token Programme is a unique initiative aimed at giving back to the community and supporting local good causes. The programme operates through Asda stores across the country, where customers are invited to vote for their favorite local community group or charity. Each store nominated three local organizations, and customers are given a green token at the checkout to cast their vote for the cause they wish to support.

The Green Token Programme is an important part of Asda’s commitment to supporting the communities in which they operate. By giving customers a say in where the store’s support goes, the programme empowers local communities and helps to make a real difference to the lives of the people in the area. The programme has provided crucial funding for a wide range of local organizations, from youth groups and sports clubs to environmental initiatives and health charities. It has become a key part of Asda’s efforts to support local communities and make a positive impact where it is needed most.

This win is a testament to the hard work and dedication of the Safe Havens team and the support they have received from the local community. The donation from ASDA will help our charity continue its important work, providing a safe and supportive environment for those in need.

This win highlights the importance of supporting local charities and the positive impact they can have in the community. Pillow Safe Havens is a shining example of the positive difference that can be made by working together and supporting those in need.

A Thanks From CEO, Scott Weir

“We are incredibly grateful for the support we have received from the local community through the Green Token Giving Programme. We are deeply grateful and immensely honored to be granted this donation. This donation will help us to continue our work, providing a safe haven for those who need to most and helping people in the local community. We are thrilled to have our efforts recognized in this way. Thank you to the Asda Green Token Giving Programme for giving us the opportunity to help our local community on a greater level. Finally a big thanks to the Pillow/ Safe Havens team for all their hard work, dedication and passion.”

Pillow Safe Havens would like to express our sincerest thanks to Glenn from Pillow HQ, who played a key role in making this award possible. Glenn has demonstrated a remarkable dedication to our cause over the past months, most recently organising a successful food donation last month to supply those in need with a warm Christmas dinner. He truly is an invaluable member of the Safe Havens Team and we are grateful to have him on board.

Safe Havens intend to utilize this generous charitable donation in a manner that will benefit the broader community in the best way. Keep an eye on our social media channels to stay updated on our journey.
