Kilt Walk

  • Home

Take part in Scotland's favourite mass participation walking event

Since 2016, approximately 120,000 people have participated in Scotland's largest mass walking event, Kiltwalk. The charitable Kiltwalk community has made great efforts for charities This year Safe Havens is taking part to raise funds for the charity to help causes dear to our hearts. Kiltwalk has so far succeeded in raising $24.6 million. With the addition of £12.5 million from Sir Tom Hunter and The Hunter Foundation, a stunning total of £37 million has been raised and distributed to 3,000 Scottish Charities.

Anybody can take part, have an amazingly fun day all while helping to support our charitible work
