Safe Havens pledge to St Mirren Charitable Foundation

Pillow Safe Haven’s have recently donated £500 to The amazing St Mirren Charity, an organization headed by St Mirren Football Club committed to implementing a positive change in the community.

The foundation helps a vast range of groups in a number of ways, from vulnerable young people to isolated older individuals, the charity doesn’t discriminate. This year alone they have donated to food banks, created a community space for men feeling isolated and continued fighting the war on mental health by hosting free mental fitness classes.

Most recently they held a 12 days of Christmas charity auction to raise money for people in need this Christmas where items such as tickets to see hit DJ Ewan McVicar and a signed away strip, worn at the legendary music festival Creamfields were auctioned off.

Pillow Safe Havens are looking to match our £500 donation. If just 50 people donate £10 each we can match this pledge to help even more people in the community.

We encourage you to donate to this amazing charity who are transforming local communities and making a life changing difference
